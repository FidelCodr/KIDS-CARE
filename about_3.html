About Us

Communities of Rethabiseng, Refilwe and Zithobeni in the Northern-East of Pretoria are equipped to care, support and protect their children. The children in these communities are aware of their rights, their responsibilities and are stakeholders in the processes that pertain to their holistic wellbeing. These communities are also thriving with healthy and economically independent people. The people of these communities have an understanding of HIV/AIDS and live lives that reduce the risks of getting HIV/AIDS. Those who have got HIV/AIDS and their families are supported and accepted by the community

About Us

KCST’s Aims

  • To increase people’s awareness around HIV/AIDS in the community so that they can make preventative and well informed decisions about their lifestyles.
  • To assist and support children to improve their academic grades so that they can get tertiary acceptance and possibly financial aid,
  • To advocate for and raise awareness in the communities around children’s rights so that the community can care for and protect its children
  • To build the skills capacity and improve the economic capacity of the youth and the community at large so that they can become economically independent,
  • To assist people in the communities to find help when KCST cannot help them.

Recent Programmes

KCST is a fully-fledged drop in centre with safe park and ECD facilities. The organisation caters for five hundred and 30 children per month of which hundred and ten of them are living with HIV and Aids disease.
