Programmes

KCST renders services to children orphaned or made vulnerable as a result of HIV/AIDS. The services are aimed at counteracting the devastating effects of HIV/AIDS focusing on the immediate needs of the child. Most children lose their security when a parent dies and experience a tremendous sense of loss, anxiety, hopelessness and disempowerment.
