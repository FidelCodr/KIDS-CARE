KIDS CARE AND SUPPORT TRUST

More Info...

Kids Care and Support Trust (KCST) is a Non-Profit Organization (NPO) that was registered in December 2003 under the auspices of St Joseph’s Care and Support Trust and became independent entity in September 2008. The organization initially was taking care of children whose parents had been admitted at the Hospice of St Joseph’s Care and Support Trust. The need for similar services was identified in the surrounding communities. It was decided to expand the service to include the communities at large. The organisation operated as a pilot Drop-in-centre model for the National Department of Social Development under mentorship of St Joseph’s for five years before the trust was registered and started functioning independently.
