    CARE AND SUPPORT TRUST

    Kids Care and Support Trust (KCST) is a community based organisation in Bronkhorstspruit, Tshwane Region 7. KCST was founded in the year 2003 2003 by Mrs. Schilling from St Joseph’s Care and Support Trust (St Joseph’s) as a pilot drop in Centre model for National Department of Social Development.     4. Other special projects implemented by the KCST

    KCST’s Comprehensive Vision Communities of Rethabiseng, Refilwe and Zithobeni in the Northern-East of Pretoria are equipped to care, support and protect their children. The children in these communities are aware of their rights, their responsibilities and are stakeholders in the processes that pertain to their holistic wellbeing. These communities are also thriving with healthy and economically independent people. The people of these communities have an understanding of HIV/AIDS and live lives that reduce the risks of getting HIV/AIDS. Those who have got HIV/AIDS and their families are supported and accepted by the community.

    WE ARE
    NONPROFIT TEAM

    KCST as a community based project started due to the masses of children left orphaned and/or vulnerable due to the devastating effects of the HIV/AIDS pandemic. Our drop in centre came in; to give as much needed support to the community and especially the children and to create an environment for children to realize their full potential.

    Our Blog

    KCST is a fully-fledged drop in centre with safe park and ECD facilities. The organisation caters for five hundred and 30 children per month of which hundred and ten of them are living with HIV and Aids disease.
